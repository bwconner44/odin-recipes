Mom's Chili

Thick and Spicy Chili

This recipe is a family secret from my mother. She used to make this for my dad when I was growing up. I never ate it growing up because it was too spicy for me. As I got older and began expanding my palate, I began liking more and more spicy dishes. I eventually asked my mother for the recipe one year in college. I have made it multiple times every fall.

This recipe produces a very thick recipe. The recipe states that, when the chili is finished cooking, a spoon should be able to stand staright up. This is the way that I like my chili. It is more stew like than soup like. This recipe also is jammed packed of spices. The spices build is fairly simple and common, but there is a heavy amount of chili pepper and red pepper. The amount listed is the amount that is comfortable for me to eat without feeling like I am sweating as much as I do after a marathon. The spice build can be adjusted up or down depending on your heat tolerance.
